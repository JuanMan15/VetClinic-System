## 🏥 About

This application is designed to efficiently manage veterinary clinic operations, meeting the needs of administrators, employees, and others. It allows management of pets, appointments, products, among other implementations.

---

## 🚀 Main Features

### 🐕 Pet Management
- Add pets to the database with their respective data and images
- View a gallery of pets with their images and detailed information
- User, sales, stock and role management for administrators

### 👥 User Management
- Register and log in as client, employee, or administrator
- Manage user profiles, including profile picture updates
- Administrators can view employees with their personal data, sales history, and profile picture

### 📅 Appointment Management
- Schedule appointments for pets specifying:
  - **Date:** Example: `22-11-2024`
  - **Service:** Example: `Bath and haircut`
  - **Weight and age of the pet**
  - **Pet's name**
  - **Notes:** Example: `The pet is allergic...`
  - **Pet images**
- Employees manage appointments only for assigned pets
- Administrators manage all appointments without restrictions

### 📦 Product Management
- Display products available at the veterinary clinic
- Both employees and administrators can process sales
- Administrators can create products with:
  - **Name**
  - **Description**
  - **Category**
  - **Price**
  - **Product image**
- Administrators can delete products and update inventory quantity

---

## 👔 Roles & Permissions

### ⭐ Administrator
- **Dashboard:** View all pets with their appointments
- **Appointment Management:** Create appointments and related pets
- **Product Management:** Create/edit/delete products and manage stock
- **Sales:** Access sales section to view transactions
- **User Management:** View employees with images, personal data, and sales performance

### 👨‍💼 Employee (Regular User)
- **Dashboard:** View only pets they assigned for appointments
- **Appointment Management:** Create appointments for assigned pets
- **Products:** View all products and process sales (no delete/stock update permissions)

### 🖼️ Profile Pictures
Both employees and administrators can update their profile picture within the application.
